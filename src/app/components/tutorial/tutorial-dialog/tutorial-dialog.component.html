Welcome to parkabler!

You can use this app to find and navigate to handicapped accessible parking spots.

You can also add new spots that we don't know about yet and report spaces with incorrect information.

Let's get started!

Setting Destination

You can search for any location that you want to go to from the search bar.

You can also find parking near you by selecting 'Near Me'.

Navigating

Once you know where you want to go, just tap 'directions' and get going with your favorite navigation app.

If you find that this spot data is innacurate, please hit 'report' so that we can correct the information.

Thank you for your help!

Adding Spots

We rely on your help to keep our data up to date! If you see a spot that's not on our map, please share it by clicking 'Add Spot'.

Drag the map around so that the pin is centered over the location of the parking spot, and add details.

Thank you for your help!
